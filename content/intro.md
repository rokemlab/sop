Standard Operating Procedures
=============================

This website outlines the standard operating procedures for the [Rokem Neuroinformatics R&D Group (NRDG)](https://rokemlab.github.io) at the Krembil Centre for Neuroinformatics.

The website includes information about our mission, vision and values, as well as practical information about how we do our research.
